Our Story

Broke N Built Services was founded with a simple mission: transform damaged, outdated, and incomplete spaces into functional, modern, and visually impressive environments. What started as a small renovation team has grown into one of Bangalore's trusted names in space transformation.

Over the past 12+ years, we've completed 150+ projects across residential, commercial, and workspace segments. Our approach combines technical expertise with creative design thinking, ensuring every project is delivered with strength, style, and purpose.

We believe that every space has potential. Whether it's a damaged structure needing complete restoration or an outdated interior ready for a modern upgrade, we bring the same dedication, attention to detail, and commitment to quality.
